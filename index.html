// how to download
STEP 01
Click the download button
Find the file you want and click the green Download button on its card.
STEP 02
You'll be redirected
The button will take you to Mega.nz or Google Drive where the file is hosted.
STEP 03
Download from there
On Mega click the cloud/download icon. On Google Drive click the download arrow at the top right.
STEP 04
Having issues?
If a link is broken or a file won't download, tell the site owner to update the link.
